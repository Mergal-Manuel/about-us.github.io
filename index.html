About Us

Welcome to Mergal's Kawali, your home for discovering and celebrating the rich flavors of traditional Filipino cooking. Here, we bring together generations of recipes passed down from lola's handwritten notes to nanay's favorite dishes served on a Sunday afternoon. Our mission is simple: to preserve the essence of Filipino culture and its heartfelt connection to home-cooked meals.

We believe that food is more than just sustenance—it's a story, a memory, and a bond between loved ones. Whether you're learning to make your first Sinigang, Adobo, or exploring the comforting warmth of Champorado, Mergal's Kawali is here to guide you. Our platform is lovingly crafted for every Filipino, whether at home or abroad, longing for the tastes that feel like home.

Join us as we share recipes, and a love for the culinary traditions that define our identity as Pinoy. Let’s celebrate the joy of Filipino food—one kawali at a time.
